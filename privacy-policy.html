TaskaChat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

TaskaChat ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the TaskaChat mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username
  • Display name
  • Profile picture
  • Email address (if provided)
  • User ID

Connection Information

  • Connection requests
  • Accepted connections
  • Group memberships
  • Public discussion activity

Messages

TaskaChat stores messages exchanged between connected users to provide chat functionality.

QR Information

TaskaChat generates and processes QR codes used for user connections.

Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Enable messaging and connections
  • Process QR-based connections
  • Provide groups and public discussions
  • Deliver notifications
  • Improve app performance
  • Prevent abuse and fraud
  • Maintain platform security

3. Phone Numbers

Your phone number, if collected, is not publicly displayed to other users.

4. Public Discussions

Information posted in Public Discussions may be visible to other TaskaChat users.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Google Play Services
