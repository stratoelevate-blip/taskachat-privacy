TaskaChat Child Safety Standards

TaskaChat is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitment

We prohibit any content, activity, or behavior that exploits, harms, abuses, endangers, or sexualizes children.

Users are not permitted to:

  • Upload, share, distribute, request, or promote child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Engage in child grooming or exploitation.
  • Use TaskaChat to facilitate trafficking, abuse, or exploitation of minors.
  • Encourage or promote harmful activities involving children.

Reporting and Enforcement

TaskaChat provides mechanisms for users to report content, accounts, groups, discussions, messages, or behavior that may violate our policies.

Reported content may be reviewed and appropriate action may be taken, including:

  • Content removal.
  • Account suspension.
  • Permanent account termination.
  • Reporting to relevant authorities where required by law.

Compliance

TaskaChat complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with authorized law enforcement agencies when legally required.
