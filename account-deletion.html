TaskaChat Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your TaskaChat account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to:

stratoelevate@gmail.com

Include:

  • Your TaskaChat username
  • The email associated with your account (if applicable)
  • A request stating that you want your account deleted

What Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Connection information
  • QR identity information
  • Groups and participation records where applicable

Data Retention

Some information may be retained when required by law, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate business obligations.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Contact

Email: stratoelevate@gmail.com
