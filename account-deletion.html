Request Account Deletion

To delete your TaskaChat account, send a request to our support email:

```
stratoelevate@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your TaskaChat username
  • The email associated with your account, if applicable
  • A clear request stating that you want your account deleted

What Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, TaskaChat will delete or anonymize account-related information where applicable.

  • Profile information
  • QR identity information
  • Connection information
  • TaskaHub participation records where applicable
  • User-generated content associated with your account where permitted
Data Retention

Certain information may be retained where required for legal compliance, security investigations, fraud prevention, or enforcement of our Terms of Service.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are normally reviewed and completed within 30 days.

If you have questions about your account, privacy, or data deletion, contact TaskaChat support.
```
